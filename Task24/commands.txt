Objective: Understand Azure subscription, SLA, and service lifecycle basics.

Step 1: Search for 'Subscriptions' in Azure Portal.
Step 2: Open your active Subscription (Sandbox or Student Subscription).
Step 3: Review Subscription Overview details.
Step 4: Go to 'Help + Support' section.
Step 5: Search for Azure SLA documentation.
Step 6: Read SLA for Virtual Machines and Storage services.
Step 7: Open 'Service Health' and 'Resource Health' (if available).
