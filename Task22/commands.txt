Objective: Monitor Azure resources using Azure Monitor and Metrics.

Step 1: Search for 'Azure Monitor' in the portal.
Step 2: Open Azure Monitor Dashboard.
Step 3: Click on 'Metrics'.
Step 4: Select a resource like VM or Storage Account.
Step 5: View metrics such as CPU, Disk, and Network usage.
Step 6: Go to 'Activity Log' to check resource activities.
Step 7: Explore Alerts section.
